About Me

My name is Tanner Lumsden, I was born and raised in the Outer Banks of North Carolina. I lived there until I joined the US Navy at 21. I was stationed in San Diego, CA for 6 years, during which I went on deployment twice to Bahrain. My rate was CM, or Construction Mechanic, I worked mainly on Diesel equipment. I have always loved computers growing up and spend a majority of my free time on them, so i figured a combination of mechanic and computers came out to be software development! I am currently enrolled in the UNC Charlotte coding bootcamp and hope to find employment in this field shortly after completing this course.
